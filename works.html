Sly Serpentine Slicks is a game of sales pitches and liars. Players will take turns being the Snake Oil Salesman of the day while everyone else plays the role of Collector. A Salesman wants to pitch and pawn off an item from their hand to trade with the group. while the Collectors want to offer up items in exchange for an item they need to place on their shelves. First to fill up their shelf wins!
