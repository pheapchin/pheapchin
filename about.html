Hi! I'm Pheap Chin, a multi-discipline artist and game developer currently studying at Pratt Institute (BFA in Game Arts) in his 3rd year.


Professionally interested in character and systems design. I have a fascination with the visual development and conceptualization of fashionably dressed characters integrated into fantastical worlds. My goal is to utilize game systems in order to bring it all together for the player.
